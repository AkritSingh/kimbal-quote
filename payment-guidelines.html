30% Project Kick-off

An upfront payment of 30% is due before the engagement begins. This confirms the project slot and covers the initial discovery and planning effort.

30% After 30 Days

A second payment of 30% is invoiced once the first 30 calendar days of planned work are completed. This aligns with the milestone review and progress sign-off.

40% Final Delivery

The remaining 40% is payable at day 50, once every agreed scope item has been delivered, reviewed, and readied for handover.

Transparent Pricing

Quoted amounts cover only the services described. Third-party expenses, such as GST, statutory taxes, vendor fees, or additional freelance costs, are not included and will be discussed and approved before they are incurred.
